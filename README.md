# Datlora

Datlora is a public country data platform that connects live global news context, issue briefs, and official country indicators.

The site helps users explore country-level information related to trade, energy, food imports, fuel imports, tariffs, logistics, imports/GDP, and supply-chain exposure.

## Live site

https://datlora.com

## Main sections

- Home: overview of live news, issue watch, and country data search
- Countries: searchable country directory
- News: global news board and country-specific news links
- Issues: issue briefs for oil shock, food import exposure, tariff pressure, and supply-chain exposure
- Country pages: official indicators, source years, related issue links, and country news links
- Compare: country-to-country indicator comparison
- Sources: explanation of data source layers
- Methodology: explanation of how news context and official indicators are separated

## Data and source approach

Datlora separates external news links from official public statistics.

Country indicators are displayed from public data sources where available, including World Bank indicators and related official trade, tariff, and energy references. External news links are provided as context and are not treated as official statistical evidence.

## Technical stack

- Next.js App Router
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Vercel deployment
- Public country indicator APIs
- External news discovery and search links

## Important notes

Datlora is for informational research. It does not replace legal, investment, customs, trade, logistics, policy, financial, or business advice.

External news links are controlled by their original publishers or search providers. Official indicator years may differ by country and indicator.
